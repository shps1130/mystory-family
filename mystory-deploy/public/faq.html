About Grace & Sage
Grace is an AI guide — she's been carefully designed to ask the right questions, listen deeply, and never rush you. She's not a human writing coach or therapist, but she has been built to feel like a trusted friend who happens to be a gifted interviewer. Think of her as available any time, never in a hurry, and able to remember everything you share. The stories you tell are 100% yours — Grace just helps you find them.
Grace is your guide if faith has been a central part of your life — she speaks naturally about God, scripture, and spiritual meaning. Sage is warmly curious and deeply human, but doesn't lead with religious language. When you answer a few questions at the start, we'll introduce you to the guide who feels right for your story.

Our Promise to You

MyStory.Family is built on trust. You are sharing memories, stories, and a lifetime of experiences with us. We take that responsibility seriously. This policy explains clearly what we collect, how we use it, and what we will never do with it.

What We Collect

When you use MyStory.Family, we collect:

  • Your name and email address when you create an account
  • The stories, memories, and responses you share during your sessions
  • Photos you choose to upload to your book
  • Payment information — processed securely through Stripe (we never see your card details)
  • Basic usage data to help us improve the experience (pages visited, features used)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Save your story so you can return to it any time
  • Generate and format your book
  • Process your payment and fulfill print orders
  • Send you important account information (payment confirmations, etc.)
  • Improve the product based on how people use it

What We Will Never Do

  • We will never sell your personal information or your stories to anyone
  • We will never use your stories to train AI models
  • We will never share your stories with third parties without your explicit permission
  • We will never send you unsolicited marketing from third parties
  • We will never store your credit card information (Stripe handles this securely)

Your Stories Are Private

The memories and stories you share with Grace are yours alone. They are stored securely and associated only with your account. No one at MyStory.Family reads your stories except in rare cases where you contact us for support and explicitly share content with us to resolve an issue.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use trusted third-party services including Anthropic (AI processing), Stripe (payments), and Vercel (hosting) — all of which maintain their own rigorous security standards.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Export your stories in a readable format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@mystory.family.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary to keep you signed in and remember your preferences. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

Children's Privacy

MyStory.Family is designed for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes to this policy, we will notify you by email. Continued use of MyStory.Family after notification constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
