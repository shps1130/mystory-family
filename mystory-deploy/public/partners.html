How the Partnership Works

We do the heavy lifting. You make one introduction to your community.

1
We Build Your Page
A dedicated page at mystory.family/[yourorg] — your logo, your colors, a custom section in every book about your organization and the causes your donors love.
2
You Send One Email
We provide a ready-to-send email template and a printable flyer. One message to your community is all it takes to launch your campaign.
3
Your Community Tells Their Stories
Donors buy for themselves or as gifts. Grace — our faith-centered AI guide — walks them through their entire life story, including a section about your organization.
4
You Receive Your Share
$25 per individual sale lands in your account automatically. Monthly reports show you exactly how many stories are being written and what you've earned.
